Osho Jain
Osho Jain is an Indian Singer-Songwriter, Performer, and Music Composer/Producer. Hailing from Indore, he is currently based in Mumbai. Otherwise outspoken, Osho expresses vulnerability and his journey through music.

He incorporates a lot of Hindi/ Urdu literature and socially relevant topics in his songs as well.
Salman Elahi
Salman Elahi is a mumbai based singer-songwriter who primarily sings and writes in urdu/hindi. He's performed solo accross country at several venues.

He majorly believes in expressing his thoughts about life, self realisation and self discovery via his music.
Fiddlecraft
An Indie Pop Rock band hailing from Pune, Fiddlecraft created waves across the country when they launched their debut album “Hawai Jahaaz”.

From composing and performing multiple songs for “The Family Man Season 2”, Gaurav Kadu - the frontman and vocalist/song writer/composer of Fiddlecraft, is known for his madness and energy he spreads on live stage.
Rahgir
Rahgir’s name perfectly explains his thoughts and dreams. He is a travelling musician/ writer/ storyteller who has travelled across India, while performing at uncountable music festivals, streets, hospitals, schools, colleges, cafes, and NGOs.

He is also an author of a book “Aahil”. He plays his Original Compositions and Rajasthani, Punjabi folk songs.
Shubhank Sharma
Hailing from Rohru, Himachal Pradesh Shubhank Sharma is a self taught singer-songwriter/composer and a multi instrumentalist. He is producing his kind of music since last 2 decades And currently he is working on his next album 'Dastak'.

His music is a rock blend of blues.He is the first artist to complete a 12 city Himachal Pradesh tour in 2019.
Purvashi Grover
Purvashi Grover started singing when she was 4 years old. She is a self taught musician with over 300 stage performances. Her dedication and purity towards her music made her Awaz Punjab Di's semi finalist.
Sultan | Chizai
More than being a band, Chizai is a group of friends that come together to cheer the spirit of life and good vibes. They believe in spreading love and happiness. With the launch of their debut album "Lossless", they have transitioned from being one of the most popular cover bands in Delhi to an underground original band.

Sultan started singing by accident. Since then he never looked back. With an uncanny ear for notes and harmonies, and catching the right tone, his voice has been described by many as soulful, and fuses with ease to any genre, to that of any singers.
The Groovy Guys
The Groovy Guys’, band based from the Lucknow city who keeps on travelling across the nation and showcasing their art and music everywhere. They don't have a vocalist like every band. Their music is a fusion of the soothing sound of violin, the rock music of guitar and an EDM feel of the beatbox.
Moongphali
Moongphali is a Live Rock Band. Experience ‘Rock and Roll’ blended with multiple musical styles, perfected over the years to become a one-of-a-kind RoastedRock experience.

With a musical offering for all ages, Moongphali keeps audiences on edge with its captivating melodies and power-packed live performance.
Manas Taneja
A 20 year old Singer/Songwriter, hailing from Pune, He has just released his latest single, 'Noori', which has already been an earworm for the people around the town.

He has just entered the indie scene and already has two singles released. He plans on releasing his debut mixtape in the coming months, on which he has been working lately.Don't miss out on the moment when he gets on the stage with his guitar and loop station!
Siddhant & Anoop
From belting out beats on school desks to playing music for different theatrical groups, Anoop learnt how to play multiple instruments like drums, djembe, conga, metallophone & more, but the Power Sector professional discovered his true love lay in the unique & mesmerizing sounds of the Handpan

Accompanying him is the thunderous Siddhant Chhabra, a finance expert and also a theater artist, whose fingers have been working their magic on any and all percussion instruments for more than a decade now.

Mixing ice and fire in a beautiful amalgamation of harmony and beats, Anoop & Siddhanth leave the audience hypnotized & entranced, and once the duo begin, no one is able to keep their feet still!!

Frequently Asked Questions

How to reach the venue?

Google maps link to the venue : https://goo.gl/maps/pXhWhrNTSDd4mbYP8

Festival Bus: Enjoy to and fro bus service from Delhi to the venue at a nominal price. Travel with the artists and get to know other attendees during our journey to the venue. You can book this option during checkout.

Airports: You can take a flight to Pantnagar (Minor Airport) or Delhi.

Train Stations: Kathgodam is the nearest train station. Taxis are easily available to reach Mukteshwar from Kathgodam.

What is the cancellation policy for the festival?

95% (payment gateway/internet handling fee will not be refunded) of the booking amount will be refunded in case the festival gets cancelled due to government regulations or any unforeseen circumstances. You will also be able to roll-over 100% of the booking amount for the next edition.


We also understand that unforeseen circumstances might need you to change plans, and so our cancellation policy makes it easier to do that.

  • Cancellations before 1st October — 95% of the booking amount will be refunded.
  • Cancellations before 8th October — 50% of the booking amount will be refunded.
  • Cancellations on and after 8th October — No Refund.
